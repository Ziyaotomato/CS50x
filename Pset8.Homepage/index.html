A Bit about Me

More about Capybara

My Food

We capybara are herbivores, and we use our long, sharp teeth for grazing on grass and water plants. An adult capybara (like me!) can eat 6-8 pounds of grass per day. During the dry season, when fresh grasses and water plants dry up, a capybara will eat reeds, grains, melons and squashes.

My Friend

We capybara are good friends and we can be friends with almost every creature on earth. For example, my husband and I enjoy hanging out with ducks, hippos, crocodiles, monkeys, dogs, cats, etc. We also love human beings, especially those who give us delicious oranges and let us join them in the spring.

My Habits

We capybara love to do several things, for example:

  • Rest close together at water's edge.
  • Rest in water or mud when it's hot.
  • Gazing in the afternoon, the evening.
